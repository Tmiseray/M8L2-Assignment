About Us

At Chronic Gamers, we're all about putting the controller in your hands, on your terms.
Whether you're looking for access to a massive game library with our subscription service or prefer owning your favorite titles outright, we've got you covered.

That's why we've designed Chronic Gamers to suit binge players, team warriors, family fun seekers, or even the occasional solo adventurer.
With the option to request your favorite games through our feedback form, you're truly in control of your game time.
